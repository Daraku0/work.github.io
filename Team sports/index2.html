Team sports

By: Daniel Szili

More people = more fun!

A lot of people do not do exercises, because they think it is boring. But this is a weak excuse to not doing anything. You can find a lot of fun team sports where you can be have fun while exercising a lot. I have brought a couple of these which I really liked doing.

The World’s favorite sport.

I am not big fan of football, but only if we are talking about watching it. If I have to play it I am actually a huge fan of this game. It is competitive, you can use your ball skills or if you don’t have any (like me) you can just be a little bit rough on the opposite players and use your body. You can run a lot so staying in shape is secured. And you can chill with your teammates after the game. It is so much fun I can only recommend.

·        Fun

·        A lot of moving

·        Almost never boring

·        Keeps you in shape

Couple of friends playing football.

Basketball

This is one of my absolutely favorite sport, although I am really bad at it. If you really want lose weight or just do some cardio, this is your sport. There is no stopping in basketball, you have to constantly run, guard other player, or just simply get hoops. Almost every city has a local basketball community, because you have basketball field everywhere, so you can go down to one of these and chill with a couple of random people or with your friends. You only need a ball everything else is already added.

·        You can play it anywhere

·        You only need a ball

·        Lot of local communities

·        One of the most exhausting sport

Best sport ever.

 

Okay, finding an American football team is not the easiest thing to do especially in a foreign country because if you do not speak the language it is really hard to understand the play calls. But I had to put this on the list because this is my ALL-TIME favorite sport. I actually played it for 9 months but I had to stop, a knee injury hit me really hard.

 

But let’s talk about the sport itself. This is a very team-based sport, you can’t really carry your team alone so you have to trust your teammates. So if you have a team full of personalities you can achieve a lot of friendships trough your team. And nevertheless, this is one of the best sports to stay in shape and move a lot.

 

One play usually last 10 seconds, this doesn’t sounds much but in these 10 second you have to give your 100% every time. So by the end of the match you will be the most exhausted person ever. And because of the lot of movements you can build-up a lot of muscle and lose a tons of weight.

 

·        Fun

·        Team-oriented

·        Lot of movements

·        Best sport ever
